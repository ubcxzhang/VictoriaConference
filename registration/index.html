Registration fee is $20 for students, $200 for non-students. Registration fee is waived for all invited speakers.

We have at least $2000 funding to cover students' travel costs. The exact amount of support for an individual student depends on the number of students registering for this conference.

Please click this link to register this conference from PIMS website. If you are a student, you also have to fill out this form to qualify for the travel scholarship. The scholarship is on a first come, first serve basis and you need to attend the poster session of the conference to be qualified. More information is included in the form.

Note, according to PIMS instruction, PIMS page does not take $0 payments. So, the invited speakers can sign up, select the invited speaker option and proceed to payment. They will receive an "Payment Error" message but their entry will be recorded.
